- **Layer 2:** Tokenizes JSON/XML/HTML correctly, encode/decode lossless, 2x+ compression on semi-structured data
- **Layer 4:** Analyzes numeric ranges correctly, optimal bit-width selected, packing/unpacking verified with edge cases

- **Profiler:** Tracks 40+ metrics, bottleneck detection accurate, reports exportable
- **Streaming:** Blocks compress/decompress correctly, checksums validate, out-of-order recovery works

### Integration (Must Pass)
- **Engine:** L1‚ÜíL2‚ÜíL3‚ÜíL4 pipeline produces 2x+ compression vs v1.0
- **Profiler:** Integrated with engine, shows per-layer breakdown
- **Streaming:** Works transparently with all layers

### Performance (Should Pass by Jun 30)
- **CPU Throughput:** 50+ MB/s (vs 9.1 MB/s in v1.0)
- **GPU Throughput:** 100+ MB/s (with kernels)
- **Compression Ratio:** 1:200M+ (vs 1:100M in v1.0)
